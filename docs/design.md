Date: 20 September 2025

1) What this system does

This DSS helps with three day-to-day, structured decisions in a healthcare unit:

 -  Staff scheduling:
    Assign people to shifts so every shift meets its required coverage by role, at the lowest labor cost.

 -  Inventory for medical supplies:
    For each item, compute how much to order (EOQ) and when to reorder (ROP) using a fixed service level policy.

 -  Patient flow capacity:
    Choose the smallest number of servers/staff on duty that meets service targets (max wait time and max utilization), based on recent arrivals.
